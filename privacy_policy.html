Introduction

MeroSIP ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using MeroSIP, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Local Data (Stored on Your Device)
  • Portfolio information: fund names, SIP amounts, transaction history
  • App preferences: theme settings, portfolio names, display settings
  • NAV data: cached fund performance and historical data
Cloud Data (When Google Sign-In is Used)
  • Google account information: name, email, profile photo
  • Firebase authentication tokens
  • Synced portfolio data stored securely in Firebase Firestore
Automatically Collected
  • Device connectivity status (online/offline detection)
  • Anonymous usage analytics via Firebase (if enabled)
  • Crash reports to improve app stability

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the SIP tracking service
  • To sync your portfolio data across devices (when signed in)
  • To display current NAV and fund performance data
  • To send local notifications about SIP dates (with your permission)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob

Data Storage & Security

  • Local data is stored securely on your device using SQLite and encrypted SharedPreferences
  • Cloud data is stored in Google Firebase with industry-standard security
  • Your portfolio data in Firestore is protected by security rules that only allow access to your own data
  • We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties
  • Cloud sync is optional; the app works fully offline

Third-Party Services

MeroSIP uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase – Authentication, cloud data storage, analytics
  • Google AdMob – Advertising (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Google Sign-In – Optional account authentication

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all data stored in the app at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete your portfolio data from the app settings
  • Portability: You can export your transaction data as CSV
  • Opt-out: You can use the app entirely offline without cloud sync
  • Sign out: Signing out disconnects cloud sync; local data remains on device

Children's Privacy

MeroSIP is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app. Continued use of MeroSIP after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app feedback option or email us.

For support and privacy inquiries, use the in-app feedback option in the MeroSIP app.
