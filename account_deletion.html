Merosip Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your MeroSIP account and remove your associated data, you can do so directly inside the app by following the steps below.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Merosip app.
  2. Go to the Profile section.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Tap Delete All Data to permanently remove your account.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Your account profile will be permanently deleted.
  • Your email and authentication information will be removed.
  • Your personal preferences and app data will be deleted.

Data Retention

Some limited system logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance purposes. After this period, all associated data will be permanently deleted.
